Pronunciation & name review

Confirm how names, brands, and recurring terms are pronounced before the episode publishes. A short plain-language note is enough — no phonetic alphabet required.

How this works

Names and terms that are easy to say wrong are gathered from the episode transcript, social context, and guest profile. Confirm a pronunciation in plain language (“sounds like EYE-rin”) and it carries forward for intros, outros, and review handoff.

Written spellings stay owned by the transcript glossary. Names and links come from social context and guest profile reuse.
